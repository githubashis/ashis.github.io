Dr. Ashis Acharjee is presently serving as Assistant Professor (On Contract) in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at National Institute of Technology, Agartala. He received his PhD Degree in 2015 and M.Tech degree in 2010 from NIT Agartala. He has excellent academic record and around 14 years of teaching and research experience.
He has taught variety of subjects both in the UG and PG level viz.
Basic Engineering Thermodynamics, Applied Engineering thermodynamics, Engineering materials, Engineering Mechanics, Engineering Graphics, Analysis & synthesis of Mechanism, CFD, FEM and Alternative fuels and Energy System etc. He has supervised more than 6 M.Tech Project and 5 B Tech Project. Currently he is supervising 1 (one) M.Tech project and 2 (two) B.Tech project.
He has more than 18 research paper in International/National journals and Conferences to his credit. He is also an Associate member of the Institution of Engineers (India). His recent research interest area includes CFD and Heat Transfer, FEA, Thermal Engineering, Thermal Energy, Renewable and Sustainable Energy Studies, Modeling-Design-Simulation of Energy System etc.
